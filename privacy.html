AIP Portfolio Capture Privacy Policy

AIP Portfolio Capture is a Chrome extension for saving Amazon product page details into the AIP Portfolio dashboard. This policy explains what information the extension handles and why.

Information the extension collects

When you open the extension on an Amazon product page and choose to save a product, the extension may collect the product title, ASIN, brand, category, Amazon link, visible Amazon price, purchase price, and purchase date. This information is used to create an investment product record in your AIP Portfolio dashboard.

How information is used

The information is used only to help you track product investment cost, resale recovery, and Amazon commission payback in the AIP Portfolio dashboard.

Cloud storage

If you are signed in, saved product records are stored in the AIP Portfolio Supabase database for your cloud account. Supabase is used to authenticate your account and store your dashboard records.

What the extension does not do

  • We do not sell your data.
  • We do not use your data for advertising.
  • We do not read private Amazon account pages or order history.
  • We do not place orders or automate purchases.
  • We do not transfer your data except as needed to save it to your AIP Portfolio cloud account.

Data control

You can edit or delete product records inside the AIP Portfolio dashboard. You can also export a backup of your dashboard data from the dashboard.
